# Zero-Cost Upsell Script Template

Purpose: Sell something immediately after the first purchase to compress payback period.

---

## The Script

**[Immediately after initial purchase confirmation]**

"Congrats — you just made a great decision. Before I let you go, I have something that's going to make {initial_offer} work even faster for you.

Most people who buy {initial_offer} tell me the hardest part is {common_friction_point}. So I built {upsell_offer} specifically to eliminate that.

Here's what it does: {specific_benefit_1}, {specific_benefit_2}, and {specific_benefit_3}.

Normally this is {anchor_price}. But because you just joined and you're already committed, I'll do {upsell_price} — right now only.

This is a one-time thing. It won't be here tomorrow. Not because I'm playing games — because the economics only work when I can bundle it with your first purchase.

Want me to add it?"

---

## Key Principles
- Offer within 5 minutes of initial purchase (highest buying temperature)
- Upsell should solve a FRICTION POINT of the initial offer, not be unrelated
- Price at 30-50% of initial purchase
- One-time offer framing must be genuine (real margin constraint)
- Goal: recover 50-100% of CAC on day 1

## Customization Variables
- {initial_offer}: What they just bought
- {common_friction_point}: The #1 thing that slows their success
- {upsell_offer}: What you're adding
- {anchor_price}: Normal price for context
- {upsell_price}: Discounted bundle price
- {specific_benefit_1-3}: Three concrete outcomes
