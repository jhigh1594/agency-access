# How to Get Snapchat Ads Access as an Agency (2026 Guide)

Gen Z audiences are spending **more time on Snapchat than Facebook**, making it a non-negotiable channel for many agency clients. However, Snapchat's Ads Manager structure differs significantly from Meta or Google.

Getting agency access usually fails for two reasons:
1. Sending the wrong type of invite (Organization vs. Ad Account)
2. Misunderstanding Snapchat's strict limits on ad accounts per email address

For agencies focused on retail, e-commerce, or lifestyle brands, you cannot afford to skip this platform. This guide clarifies exactly how to get access, run campaigns, and manage client accounts without hitting administrative walls.

## Why Snapchat Ads Access is Unique

Snapchat does not use a "Business Manager" hierarchy like Meta. Instead, it uses **Organizations**.
