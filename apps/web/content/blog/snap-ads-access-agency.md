# How to Get Snapchat Ads Access from Clients (2026 Guide)

Agencies often lose billable hours chasing Snapchat Ads access. Unlike Meta or Google, Snapchat's architecture separates ad accounts from business management, creating confusion for clients who accidentally grant the wrong permissions.

**This guide covers the exact steps to request access, verify permissions, and troubleshoot the most common API errors.**

## Why Snapchat Ads Access is Different

Snapchat Ads operate on a two-tier system: **Ad Accounts** and **Business Centers**.

- **Ad Account:** Holds the budget and creative assets. This is where campaigns are built.
- **Business Center (formerly Organization):** The management layer that allows agencies to oversee multiple client accounts from a single dashboard.

Most onboarding failures happen because clients grant access to the Business Center but forget to assign specific ad account permissions, or they assign an employee role instead of an agency partner role.
