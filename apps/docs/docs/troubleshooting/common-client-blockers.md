# Common Client Blockers

Most stalled requests are operational, not technical.

## The top blockers

### Wrong contact

The link went to someone without admin permissions.

**Fix:** redirect the request to the person who can approve access.

### Missing context

The client does not know why they are being asked to authorize platforms.

**Fix:** send a short note that explains the purpose and expected time required.

### Platform confusion

The client does not understand the difference between assets, business managers, or account types.

**Fix:** share the relevant platform guide before asking them to continue.

### Incomplete ownership

The client organization itself does not have clean internal control over the account.

**Fix:** pause escalation to your team until the client resolves ownership internally.

## Operational rule of thumb

If a request is blocked for more than two follow-ups, stop repeating the same reminder. Identify whether the blocker is:

- wrong person,
- wrong expectation,
- wrong platform knowledge,
- or wrong request scope.
