# What Clients See

Clients move through one guided flow instead of receiving separate instructions for each platform.

## Core experience

- A branded request page with your agency details.
- A list of requested platforms or assets.
- Platform-specific authorization steps.
- Status confirmation once each step is complete.

## How to explain it to clients

Use plain language:

> You’ll open one secure link, review what we’re requesting, and approve the platforms one by one. You don’t need to send us passwords or screenshots.

## What usually reassures clients

- Clear agency branding.
- A visible list of requested access.
- The fact that they stay inside official platform authorization flows.
