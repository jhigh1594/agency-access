# Send Your Link

Once your request is created, the next job is getting it in front of the client contact who can actually approve access.

## Best practices

- Send the link to the person with platform admin rights.
- Include a one-line explanation of what they will do.
- Set a clear expectation for when you need completion.

## Recommended message

> We created one secure AuthHub link for your access approvals. Open the link, review the requested platforms, and complete the authorization steps in one flow.

## Before you send

- Confirm the requested platforms are correct.
- Double-check the client name and branding.
- Make sure the contact email belongs to the right decision-maker.

## If the client asks what happens next

- They open the link.
- They authorize the requested platforms.
- You track progress from the AuthHub dashboard.
