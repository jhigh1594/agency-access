# The Value Equation

## Formula

```
VALUE = (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood of Achievement) / (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

**Maximize the numerator.** Make the dream outcome vivid and the proof overwhelming.
**Minimize the denominator.** Reduce time to result and effort required.

## Levers

### Dream Outcome (Maximize)
The transformation the customer actually wants. Not the feature. The RESULT.
- "Lose 30 lbs" not "Get a meal plan"
- "10 new clients/month" not "Marketing strategy"
- Be specific with numbers and timeframes

### Perceived Likelihood (Maximize)
Proof that it actually works. In this order of power:
1. Statistical proof (87% of clients achieve X)
2. Case studies with specifics (name, situation, outcome)
3. Demonstrations (show, don't tell)
4. Guarantees (reverse the risk)

"Proof over Promise. Always."

### Time Delay (Minimize)
How long until they see results. Faster = more valuable.
- "If you can freeze time, you can charge whatever you want."
- Speed is worth a premium. Always.
- Fastest version of the result wins.

### Effort & Sacrifice (Minimize)
How much work the customer has to do.
- DFY (Done For You) > DWY (Done With You) > DIY (Do It Yourself)
- Each step up the effort ladder reduces what you can charge
- "Make it so easy they feel stupid not doing it"

## Application

When assessing an offer, rate each component 1-3 and calculate the ratio. Above 2.0 = Grand Slam territory. Below 0.5 = rebuild the offer from scratch.
