**Research Date:** February 27, 2026

## Executive Summary

**AgencyAccess.co** is a client onboarding and credential management platform designed for digital marketing agencies. Its core value proposition is replacing manual OAuth setup processes (which typically take 2-3 days) with a streamlined 5-minute link-based flow. The platform enables agencies to collect client credentials for Meta (Facebook/Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok, LinkedIn, and other marketing platforms through a single branded link.

**Key Finding:** AgencyAccess.co is an established competitor based in the Netherlands (AgencyAccess B.V.) with **500+ agencies** and **transparent pricing** published at /pricing. They offer client intake forms, Zapier integration, and custom branding across all tiers.

---

## 1. What They Offer

### Product Positioning
AgencyAccess.co positions itself as a **"Credentials Made Simple"** platform that solves the persistent pain point of client onboarding for marketing agencies.

### Core Value Proposition
- **1-click client onboarding** - Send one link, clients authorize all requested platforms
- **Permanent access** - Even if agencies stop using AgencyAccess, previously granted access remains intact
- **Platform approval** - Claims to be "approved by Meta, TikTok, Google, and LinkedIn"
- **Security-first** - 100% GDPR compliant, secure credential handling

### Supported Platforms
According to their homepage, AgencyAccess supports **20+ popular services** including:
- Meta (Facebook, Instagram, Facebook Ads)
- Google (Google Ads, GA4/Google Analytics)
- TikTok (TikTok Ads)
- LinkedIn (LinkedIn Ads)
- Snapchat (Snapchat Ads)
- And 15+ other platforms

### Key Features
1. **Custom Branding** - Agencies can customize invites with their logo, colors, and messaging
2. **Language Localization** - Support for multiple languages in client invites
3. **Permission Management** - Central dashboard to manage and adjust client permissions
4. **One-Link Access** - Single branded URL for all platform authorization requests
5. **No Password Sharing** - Secure OAuth-based flow, no credentials exchanged
6. **Quick Setup** - Claims "start with free plan or 30-day free trial, no credit card required"

### How It Works (4-Step Process)
1. **Connect Accounts** - Agency connects their own accounts to AgencyAccess
2. **Customize Branding** - Add logo, colors, custom messaging
3. **Invite Clients** - Send branded access request link
4. **Manage Access** - Control and adjust permissions from central dashboard

## 5. Weaknesses & Limitations (Competitive Opportunities)

### Legitimate Weaknesses for Comparison

1. **Invite Limits on Starter Tier**
   - Only 5 invites per month on $44/mo plan
   - Growing agencies may hit this quickly
   - Requires upgrade to Premium ($99/mo) for unlimited

2. **Team Member Caps**
   - Starter: 3 team members max
   - Premium: 10 team members max
   - Only Agency tier ($199/mo) has unlimited team

3. **EU-Based Company**
   - AgencyAccess B.V. is in Netherlands
   - Support hours may not align with US business hours
   - Potential time zone challenges for US agencies

4. **Limited Email Marketing Platform Support**
   - No Klaviyo, Kit (ConvertKit), or Beehiiv listed
   - Email agencies may need separate tools for those platforms

5. **No Mentioned Enterprise Security**
   - No SOC2 certification mentioned
   - No Infisical or enterprise secrets management mentioned
   - No API access documented

6. **Platform Coverage Gaps**
   - No Pinterest Ads support listed
